About Me

Hello, My name is Hero Vuth. I am a recent graduate at University of Washington Tacoma. While studying finance, I have taken a lot of interest in understanding the financial markets, the finance industry, and the global economy. However, I found the most interesting aspect of finance was financial-statement analysis. It was interesting how nuanced the work is and that there is no particular correct answer, but how you got to your answer was the most important. So, after graduating, I sought more resources to become more proficient at the fundamentals, financial modeling, and general Excel abilities.

Even though analysis is one of my most refined skills, I still have an intellectual interest in economics and finance. It is an intellectual passion of mine that I will continue to pursue to grow and refine my skill set. My ambition is reflected currently on my recent certificate from WallStreetPrep. The training from WSP revolved around financial analysis catered toward investment banking. It will be one of many certificates I will continue to pursue. However, my goal is to work in the investment side or business strategy side of finance. As for now, my current abilities are a stepping stone for me as I navigate through the industry.
